About Us

You will find The Haukai Restaurant nestled in a typical New Zealand forest with the 'greenest of green' New Zealand Silver fern trees. We specialise in Māori and Pākeha kai and include small parts of Te Ao Māori culture like karakia to show thanks for our food.
Your hosts: Nui & Mandy welcome you to their idea of paradise, one we are sure you will admire and love too. We have been enjoying and sharing delicious food in this small part of paradise for nearly a decade and invite you to try it out!!!
Haere Mai.
